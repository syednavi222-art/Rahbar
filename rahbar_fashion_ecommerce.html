Our Mission

Quality Without Compromise

Founded in 2024 in Karachi, RAHBAR was born from a simple frustration: Pakistani fashion-conscious youth had to choose between expensive imported brands or low-quality local alternatives.

We changed that. RAHBAR bridges the gap — offering international-grade quality, contemporary design, and affordable prices, all rooted in Pakistani pride.

Home / FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RAHBAR

📦 Orders & Shipping

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 business days) is also available at extra cost. Delivery times may vary based on your city and availability. Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad typically receive orders faster.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
Yes! COD is available nationwide across Pakistan. You pay when you receive your order. No advance payment required. COD is our most popular payment option!
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via SMS and/or WhatsApp. You can use this to track your parcel through our courier partner's website.
Do you ship to all cities in Pakistan?
Yes! We deliver nationwide to all major and minor cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, Sialkot, Hyderabad, and hundreds more.

↩️ Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return and exchange policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Please contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Absolutely! Size exchanges are our most common request and we make them easy. Contact us within 7 days of delivery and we will arrange an exchange. Shipping charges for exchange are ₨200.

👕 Products & Sizing

How do I know which size to order?
Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements in centimeters. We recommend measuring your chest and comparing with the size chart. When in doubt, size up for a more relaxed fit.
What fabric/material do you use?
We use premium ring-spun cotton (240–320GSM depending on the product), French terry for hoodies and sweatshirts, and premium polyester blends for tracksuits. All materials are tested for durability and comfort.
Home / Return & Exchange Policy

Return & Exchange Policy

At RAHBAR, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to return or exchange your item(s). After 7 days, unfortunately we cannot offer a return or exchange.

Conditions for Return

  • Item must be in original condition — unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Item must be in original packaging
  • Proof of purchase required (order number)

Non-Returnable Items

  • Sale items (unless defective)
  • Accessories (caps, bags) for hygiene reasons
  • Items damaged by misuse

How to Return

Contact us on WhatsApp at +92-311-RAHBAR with your order number and reason for return. We will guide you through the simple process. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after receiving the returned item.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

RAHBAR ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

How We Use Your Information
